Host Controls & Guidance
  • Starting a meeting will open Zoom's host page or native client. For scheduled meetings create them in your Zoom account and paste the link here.
  • Enable Waiting Room or a passcode to protect meetings with sensitive patient data.
  • To record the session, enable recording in Zoom and inform the patient in advance.
  • To embed meetings on-site consider Zoom Web SDK or Video SDK; they require server-side signing and API keys.
