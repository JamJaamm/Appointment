Helpful tips
  • Copy the full Zoom meeting URL if your doctor shared one (it usually looks like https://zoom.us/j/1234567890?pwd=...).
  • If you don't have a meeting link, ask your doctor to share the Meeting ID and Passcode.
  • The "Open in Zoom App" button will attempt to open the native Zoom client using the meeting ID. Some browsers may block this and prompt you.
  • For the best experience use Chrome or Edge and ensure your microphone and camera are allowed.

Security & privacy notes

When you join a Zoom meeting from this page we redirect you to Zoom's official web client or native app. We do not capture or record audio/video on this site. If you need recording enabled, ask the meeting host (doctor) to enable it in Zoom settings.
